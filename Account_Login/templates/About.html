About Cake n Bake

Welcome to Cake n Bake – the brand that is synonymous with all kinds of celebrations.

Birthdays, proposals, baby showers or farewells, weddings or anniversaries, Cake n Bake will help you create them effortlessly. From delivering all the required things for your event right at your doorstep to helping you execute an unforgettable experience from start to finish, we will be with you throughout the process. Our vision is to have people ‘think Cake n Bake’ when they think celebrations. Our mission is to provide best quality products, timely services and most importantly, a wholesome customer experience. From inspirational ideas to stunning productions, strong themes and creative programs, you can trust us at Cake n Bake, to create memorable experiences that exceed your expectations. We are committed to create an enhanced gifting system and provide a one-stop solution to make gifting an informed, hassle-free and delightful experience. We strive to create value, not Valuation. When you order from Cake n Bake, whether from our website or over the phone, you can be rest assured that your wish is our command. Because we look at each event as a custom made project irrespective of size or budget, we believe that you will let us exhibit our passion and dedication to stage your event with a guarantee to surpass your expectations!

THE CHEF



Vatsal Maheshwari
